# EEL-Data-Analytics-Project
Dairy data analysis project focusing on sales, expiry risk, and inventory using Excel and Tableau.

# 🥛 Dairy Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes dairy product data to understand sales trends, demand patterns, inventory health, and expiry risk. The analysis focuses on deriving insights that can help improve decision-making in a dairy supply business.

---

## 📊 Dataset & Sheets

The project is organized into the following data sheets:

* **csv_data** – Raw dataset
* **formatted_data** – Cleaned and structured data
* **demand_vs_prod** – Comparison of product demand and supply
* **profit_margin** – Analysis of pricing and revenue performance
* **inventory_health** – Stock levels, reorder points, and efficiency
* **shelf_life_and_waste** – Expiry risk and product lifespan analysis
* **revenue_by_sales_channel_&_customer_location** – Revenue trends across channels and regions

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze demand across products and locations
* Evaluate revenue and profit patterns
* Monitor inventory efficiency and stock levels
* Understand expiry risk and product shelf life
* Identify areas for reducing waste and improving sales

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

* Demand vs Product Availability
* Profit Margin Analysis
* Inventory Health (Stock vs Sales vs Reorder)
* Shelf Life vs Expiry Risk
* Revenue by Sales Channel and Customer Location

---

## 📈 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables)
* Tableau (Data Visualization Dashboard)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* High-demand products are not always aligned with stock levels, indicating inventory inefficiencies
* Refrigerated products tend to have higher expiry risk due to shorter shelf life
* Revenue varies significantly across sales channels and customer locations
* Pricing plays a key role in influencing product demand

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

The project highlights important relationships between demand, pricing, inventory, and product lifespan. These insights can help optimize stock management, reduce waste, and improve overall business performance.
